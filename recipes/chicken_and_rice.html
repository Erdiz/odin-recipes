Chicken and Rice

Description

Ever wanted to create chicken that reminds you of your favorite mexican place, while saving money and making it healthier(because everything homemade is healthier)? This recipe recreates that mexican chicken taste by using a simple marinate to pack the chicken full of flavor. To go with your chicken is also an included recipe for lime cilantro lime rice which will provide so much needed acidity to go with your savory and spicy chicken.

Ingredients

  • 6 Chicken Thighs
  • 1 Can of Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt/Sour Cream
  • 1/2 Yellow onion
  • 1 Lime
  • 1/2 cup of Long Grain Rice
  • 1 Handful of Cilantro
  • 2 tbsp Unsalted Butter

Steps

Chicken

  1. Combine your can of chipotle peppers(with the adobo sauce), yogurt, garlic cloves, salt, ground cumin, diced yellow onion, half of the juice of your lime, and half of your handful of cilantro into a container and blend with an immersion blender until homogenous.
  2. Place your chicken thighs into a bowl and cover with the marinate, making sure to fully coat the surface of each thigh.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and place in the fridge to marinate for 3-6 hours
  4. Once the chicken is done marinating heat a cast iron skillet to below medium on the stovetop.
  5. Cook 2 thighs at a time on the cast iron, making sure to flip every few minutes until the internal temperature registers between 170-175 degrees farenheit.

Rice

  1. Melt 1 tbsp of butter in a saucepan at medium heat.
  2. Once fully melted add a cup of water and place a lid on the saucepan.
  3. Once he water begins to boil add your rice to the saucepan
  4. When the water has evaporated to the point where rice grains are visible turn off the heat and allow the rice to sit until all liquid has evaporated(mix if necessary).
  5. Finally, while still hot, add your rice to a mixing bowl with half of the juice of your lime, 1 tbsp of butter, and half of your hanful of cilantro and mix well

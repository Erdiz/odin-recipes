Noodles

Description

Sometimes after work you're tired and don't have the energy to prepare a whole meal and clean up multiple pots and pans after but you still need an energy boost with some quick protein. This spicy peanut noodle recipe is eay to make, only requires 7 ingredients, and only requires a small pot to make. Optionally you can also add some more protein like chicken or beef but it isn't required if you're not feeling like it.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tabp Siracha Sauce
  • 1/3 cup Chili Oil
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 2 tsp Garlic Powder
    • <1i>Your Noodles of Choice

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a small pot and cook your noodles to the specifications on the packaging, or to your liking.
  2. In a bowl combine your peanut butter, chili oil, soy sauce, siracha sauce, and garlic powder.
  3. Once your noodles have finished cooking remove from the pot while saving some of the noodle water and run through cold water until no longer hot.
  4. Finally add a little bit of your noodle water into the bowl with your noodles and sauce.
